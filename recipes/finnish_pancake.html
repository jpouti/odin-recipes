Finnish Pancake

Description

A quick and easy way to make Finnish egg pancake

Try it out with strawberry jam, or with your favourite pancake toppings

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Place butter in a 9x13-inch baking dish and place in the preheating oven until melted, about 5 minutes.
  2. Beat milk, flour, eggs, sugar, and salt together in a bowl until batter is smooth. Pour melted butter from baking dish into batter and beat until smooth. Pour batter into the baking dish.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through, 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Enjoy with your favourite toppings!
